FAQ

How are we different?

The curriculum is more comprehensive and structured than any other online coding program available. The program combines the benefits of 1-1 teacher-led instruction, a students-first approach, and hands-on, project-based learning, with the convenience of the online live classroom.

Does my child get additional support?

Yes! We give regular homework assignments to supplement class time. There are tests, reviews and exercises to show mastery of the material.

What technology do you need at home?

You will need the following: 1) A computer with a keyboard, microphone, and speaker – almost all laptops bought within the last five years ago should have these; 2) A web browser installed, like Chrome (recommended) or Firefox or Safari; and 3) A reliable internet connection. Also, a headset is great so students can keep their hands free, but it isn’t required.

What is the time commitment?

Classes are for 1-hour per session. Practice work is assigned after every class which will take approximately 1-hour to complete. Additional projects and activities will be available for students to explore based on their interests and time availability.

How frequent are these classes?

These are live online classes. One session is an hour long. You can choose a batch as per your convenience.

What if something goes wrong before, during or after I have done my activities?

Where will the classes take place?

You can take these classes from anywhere as per your convenience. The class will take place on Google Meet. The link will be available in your dashboard.
